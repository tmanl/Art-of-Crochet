Process

How AOC experiences work

Step 01
Share your space & goals

Tell us who you’re hosting (corporate, school, community), how many people, and what you’d like them to feel when they leave.

Step 02
We design a session

We recommend the right format, length, and difficulty level. All yarn, tools, and setup is included.

Step 03
You host, we facilitate

We arrive early to set up, guide the experience, and leave you with a calm, connected group (and some very cool crochet).
