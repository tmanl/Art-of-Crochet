FAQ

Quick answers

Do you provide materials?

Yes — we bring what’s needed unless a special request is made.

What size groups work best?

Workshops scale well; lounges are great for 10–40 people; gatherings can be designed for larger community flows.

Can you tailor for corporate culture?

Yes — we can design a “Workplace Reset” that fits your team’s goals and vibe.

What should we NOT do? (RED emphasis)

Avoid rushing people or making it “performance-based”. Keep it relaxed and guided.
