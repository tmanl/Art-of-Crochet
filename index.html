Questions

FAQ

Do I need any crochet experience? +
Not at all! All Art of Crochet experiences are designed to be accessible for complete beginners. Our sessions are guided by Legin and the team, so you'll be stitching in no time regardless of your skill level.
What's included in each session? +
Every participant receives their own crochet hook, high-quality natural fibre yarns, and a drawstring bag to store their creations. All you need to bring is yourself and an open mind.
How many people can participate? +
Workshops accommodate between 25 and 200 people. The Crochet Lounge format is ideal for smaller groups of 10–40 people. For large-scale events, get in touch and we'll work out the best format for you.
Can you come to our office or venue? +
Yes! Legin Knits is fully mobile. We can bring Art of Crochet to your office, school, gallery, hotel, or any venue of your choosing. We partner with world-class caterers, DJs, and entertainers to fully customize the experience.
How do I book and what's the lead time? +
Fill out the contact form below or reach out via email at legin@leginknits.com. We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for corporate events, though we can sometimes accommodate shorter lead times.
Is Art of Crochet good for team building? +
Absolutely. AOC is a proven tool for team building — it fosters camaraderie, reduces stress, and unlocks creativity in a way most team activities can't. Past clients include AGO Youth, Staples, Sinai Health, and Soho House.
