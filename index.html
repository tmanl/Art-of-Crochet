FAQ

Quick answers

Do people need experience?

No — our formats are designed for beginners and first-timers. We keep it guided, calm, and achievable.

What do you bring?

Materials, tools, and a structured flow. If needed, we’ll also advise on space setup so it looks and feels elevated.

Can schools be one option (not the whole focus)?

Yes — the site is built around multiple audiences (teams, schools, communities). Schools are one offering, not the identity.

How do we book?

Use the Eventbrite link above for public events. For private bookings, you can add a contact section next (email + inquiry form).
